Digital Education



Aasha ka Paila is on a mission to provide children in need with quality education. Recognizing that many children still lack access to education, the organization focuses on equipping them with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in the technology-driven world of today.

The training offered by Aasha ka Paila covers a range of topics including basic computer knowledge, Artificial Intelligence, the Internet, and Web Designing. The sessions are interactive, hands-on, and designed to ignite a passion for learning in young minds.

Aasha ka Paila believes that education is a basic human right and that every child deserves the opportunity to learn and grow. With this in mind, the organization makes sure that its training sessions are accessible to all children, regardless of their socio-economic background.

By working together, Aasha ka Paila is confident that it can make a real difference in the lives of children and help them achieve their full potential. The ultimate goal is to see every child in the country have access to quality education and to inspire a love for technology in young minds.

Join Aasha ka Paila in its mission to bridge the digital divide and provide children with the education they deserve. Invest in the future by helping children today.
